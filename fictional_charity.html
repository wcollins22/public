SCP: Research and Development

Our goal:

Unbeknownst to most, the SCP Foundation stands between the world and mind bending anomalies that could end it.The researchers at the SCP Foundation work every day to obtain new information about the world's most dangerous creatures.

What we do:

SCP stands for Secure, Contain, and Protect. The Foundation does everything to secure its subjects, contain them, and finally protect the public from these subjects. This entails extremely dangerous operations, that often cost lives.

How to help:

Although the Foundation has nearly limitless resources, you are still welcome to donate. You can also leave tips or information about a potential anomaly.
